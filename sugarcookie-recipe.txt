Ingredients:
 - 2 cups all-purpose flour
 - 1 teaspoon baking-soda
 - 1 teaspoon baking powder
 - 1 cup butter
 - 2 cup white sugar
 - 1 egg
 - 2 teaspoons vanilla

Instructions:
 - Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
 - In a bowl mix together flour, baking soda, baking powder, egg, vanilla, sugar and butter (soften first).
 - Roll cookies into a ball and place on a baking sheet
 - Bake 8 to 10 mins in the oven or until golden brown on top
